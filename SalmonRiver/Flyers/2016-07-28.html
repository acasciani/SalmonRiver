Just steps from the headwaters of the Salmon River enjoy your stay in this 1100 sq. ft. cabin that sleeps 4-6. It is equipped with all amenities including stainless appliances in the custom designed kitchen, flat screen TV’s in each bedroom with charging stations for your electronics and a full bath and half. All linens are included for your convenience. Warm yourself on those chilly nights by the gas stove conveniently located in the spacious living room. The snowmobile trail is just around the corner and fishing access to the Salmon River is across the road at the headwaters.

This area has it all. Stream trout fishing, snowmobiling, hiking, kayaking, skiing or just a place to relax and enjoy nature. Located just outside the village of Altmar, New York this cabin is in the heart of Salmon fishing for the expert or the novice!

And while you are here visit the Salmon River Fish Hatchery, the state’s largest hatchery, maintained by the Department of Environmental Conservation just down the road!
